Payment FAQs

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption and comply with PCI DSS requirements to ensure your payment information is protected.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact our customer support for a refund.

Do you offer installment payments?

Yes, we offer installment options through PayPal Credit and certain credit card providers. Select "Pay Later" at checkout for available options.

Will I receive a receipt?

Yes, you'll receive an email receipt immediately after your purchase, and you can access all your receipts in your account dashboard.
